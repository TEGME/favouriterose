What Will You Get From This Book?

Mankind has been troubled with many devastating predicaments— such as climate change and poverty and hunger and the violation of human rights, just to name a few! It is now up to us who are living in prosperity to find the solutions to bring usefulness to those people in need. Hopefully, with help from God, “The Colour of Your Favourite Rose” has now become one outcome of this important endeavour.

See why Climate change is the most prominent issues of today

there are huge carbon footprints left behind by many countries. Many of us are technologically advanced yet we do not understand severe climate change! How can a nation as advanced as the United States opt out of the Global Climate Change Initiative?

Understand the traps of the modern credit culture

Everyone should know there are rules of credit, mainly for those who live in our credit-culture. If we are ignoring these rules of credit we may end up paying a penalty while not enjoying any benefits at all!

Understand why the current political system is broken

We now know that very rich people and politicians don’t always hear our honest pleas for attention. But why is that? We will discuss why our current system is so broken and how it effects our growth.

Realize the true purpose of literature and poetry

Good literature and philosophical musings cleanse our hearts and make them stronger for our happiness and a more meaningful life!

Discover the CBFD theory

The name of our evergreen thought is our “Creative Balance for Development.” In a short form we call this the “CBFD” theory to define this dynamic economic plan to bring about financial liberation.

Be inspired to make a change in yourself and the world

Let us now build positive changes through a business organization such as “TEGME", by utilzing knowledge and literature.

About The Author

Mohammed Mizan, with his talent for writing and his dedication is helping to solve our global financial crisis. He innovated and unveiled his “Creative Balance for Development theory” in 1992. Since then, he has travelled to some countries to see and experience people’s lives and their socio-economic systems which illuminate their lives.

With these goals in mind, in 2013 he launched his first book of poems, Oshojjo. In the same year he also released his first music album, Kal Ghum Vangata. In 2015, he released his second music album, Blessing. The Voices 2018, 2019, and 2020 contain six of his poems.

Presently, he continues to spread his message through poetry, short stories, and songs mostly through the tegme literary show. Now his deepest intention remains to help to stop climate change by bringing financial freedom to everyone.

He currently lives in Toronto with his wife and three daughters.
